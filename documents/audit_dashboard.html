Executive Business Summary

Overall, your website provides a professional first impression, but several technical and user experience issues are likely impacting search visibility, visitor trust, and conversion rates. Addressing these areas can improve loading speed, strengthen your online credibility, increase organic traffic, and generate more qualified business inquiries.

Immediate Critical Issues

Staging URL Link Leak: Menu links direct live site users away to insecure developer testing domains (nimeshdetroja.co.in).
Mixed Content Flags: Standard logos loading via insecure HTTP, triggering browsers' padlock warnings.
Broken Blog Grid: A layout bug causes the `/blog/` page to display items off-grid with massive empty voids.
Bad H1 SEO Tag: Homepage H1 points to "Blogs" rather than your core manufacturing headlines.

Design & Branding Review

What Works Well

  • Good Color Palette: Corporate blue (#203a71) represents trust in global supplier sourcing.
  • Modern Fonts: Clean integrate load of Google Fonts Poppins and Roboto.
  • Clear Desktop Headers: Key services and menus are legible and distinct.

Areas for Improvement

  • Clashing Logo bounding boxes: The main logo has a solid white background block that clashes visual elements in the header.
  • Broken Grid Spacing: Huge alignment voids on custom pages, notably the blogs list block.
  • Unsecured Staging URL dependencies: Logo points to an HTTP external staging URL.
